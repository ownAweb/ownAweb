Common Questions

Here are answers to some frequently asked questions

What's your typical process?

I start with understanding your goals and audience, then move to concepts, design, and revisions until I get it right. I maintain clear communication throughout.

How long does a project take?

It depends on the scope. A simple website might take 2-3 weeks, while a complete brand identity could take 4-6 weeks. We'll provide a timeline during our initial discussion.

Do you work with international clients?

Yes, I work with clients worldwide. We're based in India but available for remote collaboration across all time zones with flexible communication schedules.

What are your rates?

I work on a project basis. After discussing your needs, I'll provide a detailed quote with clear deliverables and timelines that fit your budget.

What's included in your web design service?

My web design packages typically include strategy, design, development, responsive implementation, basic SEO setup, and post-launch support for 30 days.

Do you provide ongoing maintenance?

Yes, I offer maintenance plans to keep your website updated, secure, and performing optimally after launch.
